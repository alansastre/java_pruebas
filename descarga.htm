🫀 Significado: ¿Qué significa?

The anatomical heart emoji 🫀 appears as a realistic depiction of a human heart, generally in shades of red and pink. It includes details such as the heart’s ventricles, arteries, and veins.
This emoji represents the human heart in its anatomical form and can signify life, health, cardiology, and love in a more literal sense than the traditional heart emoji ❤️. It tends to be used in medical contexts or to represent a real, raw, and deep form of love, rather than an affectionate or casual love symbolized by the standard heart emoji ❤️.

Ejemplos de texto:

  • Exercise regularly to keep your heart and body happy and healthy. 🫀💪
  • You have my heart, in the sincerest and truest way possible. 🫀❤️
